To make a unit vector, divide the elements of the vector by its length.
Formula \vec e = \frac{\vec a}{|| \vec a ||}

First, we calculate the length of \vec a by taking the square root of the dot product of the vector with itself.

\color{#6495ED}{||\vec a||} = \sqrt{showDot(VA,VA)}

\color{#6495ED}{||\vec a||} = \sqrt{showHalfdot(VA,VA)}

\color{#6495ED}{||\vec a||} = \sqrt{DOT1}

Then, we divide each element in \color{#6495ED}{\vec a} by \sqrt{DOT1}:

\frac{\color{#6495ED}{\vec a}}{\color{#6495ED}{||\vec a||}} = \frac{writeVector(VA)}{\sqrt{DOT1}}
